# Google Play Store Analysis Report

## Introduction
This project analyzes the Android app ecosystem using Google Play Store data.

## Data Preparation
- Converted numeric columns
- Cleaned pricing data
- Removed inconsistencies

## Analysis Performed
- Category distribution
- Rating trends
- Free vs Paid apps
- Popularity analysis using reviews

## Findings
- Free apps dominate the market
- Education and Game apps show higher ratings
- Popular apps tend to maintain ratings above 4.0

## Conclusion
The Google Play Store is heavily competitive with a focus on free applications.
User engagement plays a major role in app success.
